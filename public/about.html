Our Journey Through Time

Founded in 2015, Whispering Souls began as two passionate photographers with a single camera and a dream. What started in a small Mumbai studio has blossomed into an award-winning team of visual storytellers.

We've documented over 500 love stories across India, each teaching us something new about capturing raw, authentic emotion. Our evolution mirrors Indian wedding photography's journey - from traditional portraits to the cinematic storytelling we pioneer today.
